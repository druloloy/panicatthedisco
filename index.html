Discovery

Panic! at the Disco is the solo project of American musician Brendon Urie. It was initially a pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, shaped in 2004 by cherished companions Brendon Urie, Ryan Ross, Spencer Smith and Brent Wilson. They recorded their first demos while they were in secondary school. Not long after, the band recorded and delivered their presentation studio collection, A Fever You Can't Sweat Out (2005). Advocated continuously single, "I Write Sins Not Tragedies", the collection was guaranteed triple platinum in the US.
